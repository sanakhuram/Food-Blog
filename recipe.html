Delicious Recipe

Learn how to make this delicious dish step by step.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces Salmon Filet
  • ½ Onion (sliced)
  • ½ pound Oyster Mushrooms (or mushroom of your choice, sliced)
  • 3 cups Baby Spinach Leaves (loosely packed)
  • 3 tablespoons Olive Oil (divided)
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • ¼ cup White Wine
  • 1 teaspoon Fresh Garlic (minced)
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the salmon filets with salt and pepper.
  3. In a large ovenproof skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the salmon filets and sear until golden brown on both sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil and sauté the onion until translucent.
  5. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  7. Pour in the white wine and cook until it reduces by half.
  8. Add the spinach leaves and cook until wilted.
  9. Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Return the salmon filets to the skillet and spoon some of the sauce over them.
  11. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through.
  12. Remove from the oven and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
